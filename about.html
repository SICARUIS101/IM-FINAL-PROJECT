About ELE

ELE Technical Training and Assessment Center is dedicated to providing high-quality technical education and skills training. We offer a wide range of TESDA-accredited courses, hands-on learning experiences, and career development opportunities for our students. Our mission is to empower learners with the practical skills they need to succeed in today’s workforce.

What This Website Is All About

This website is designed to provide easy access to information about ELE’s programs, services, and enrollment procedures. Users can register for courses, contact administrators, and stay updated with announcements. Whether you're a student, instructor, or guest, the platform is here to help you navigate your learning journey.
