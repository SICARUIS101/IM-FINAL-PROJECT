About ELE Tech

ELE Technical Training and Assessment Center is committed to delivering high-quality technical education and hands-on training. We offer a diverse selection of TESDA-accredited courses, empowering students with the skills and experience needed to succeed in today’s workforce.

What This Website Is All About

Our website is designed to provide you with easy access to information about ELE programs, enrollment, announcements, and especially student tracking. Whether you’re a student, instructor, or visitor, this platform is built to help monitor academic progress, manage enrollment status, and guide your learning journey smoothly and efficiently.
